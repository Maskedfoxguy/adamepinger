# adamepinger
A personal resume website showcasing my professional journey, portfolio, and projects (both completed and in progress). Features include a dynamic gallery, project updates, and an overview of my skills and experiences.

This resume website is designed to:

Highlight my completed and in-progress projects.
Provide an overview of my career and professional experiences.
Include dynamic updates and recent photos.
Share insights into my skills, values, and areas of expertise.

Features:
Home Page: An introduction to who I am and my professional values.
Projects: A collection of my work, both completed and in progress, with descriptions and relevant links.
About Me: A deeper dive into my career journey, skills, and interests.
Contact Information: Ways to connect with me professionally.

Technologies I used in this project: 
HTML/CSS for layout and styling
JavaScript
Frameworks or libraries you used: React, Bootstrap.
